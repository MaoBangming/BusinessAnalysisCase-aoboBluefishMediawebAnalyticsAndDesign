How it works

Artificial Intelligence

In order for a robot to integrate into human society, it needs to have full intelligence. Using machine learning and deep learning, we have built the robot's ability to understand natural language and generate natural responses (NLU & NLG). In addition, the robot is able to recognize faces, facial expressions, objects, gestures, and its surroundings, facilitating it to construct situations so that it can interact with humans more naturally and appropriately.

Localization & Performance

Localization and mapping technology gives our robots the ability to self-locate, map building and path planning. This helps our robot to understand the layout of the home and build a spatial model that allows it to move around the house to a given location and follow instructions. High stability, high performance and high accuracy; but in such a cost effective way that our robots are affordable and can be used by anyone.

Interaction & Emotion

We believe that the interactions brought about by artificially intelligent robots can create a more intimate and warm companionship experience.Just like any living creature, we believe that robots have their own preferences. Therefore, we've built a three-dimensional emotional engine that gives each robot its own personality and autonomous control over its emotions. So, depending on its mood and environment, our robot will react differently depending on how you interact with it, and their reactions may surprise you.
